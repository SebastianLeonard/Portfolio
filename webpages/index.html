Gallery

"Illuminated Petals"

One of my very first art pieces, inspired by a game called Ender Lilies, I loved the idea of capturing a lone flower amidst a vast field, but I took a more vibrant approach. In terms of overall detail it's one of my finest works.

"Gems of the Void"

This was a quick, 1 day challenge as part of "Inktober" where many artists will create a piece based on a single word, and come up with their own creations. I kept it simple, and made some nice crystals with a general glowing effect.

"Wispy"

Another image made for "Inktober", this time the theme was spirit. I felt like creating a will-o'-wisp would've been a perfect fit. I felt like getting a bit more cutesy and childish, imitating a child's interpretation of what they saw.
